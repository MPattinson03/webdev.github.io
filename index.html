About

Hi! I’m Megan Pattinson, a student teacher at the University of Limerick, pursuing a degree in Mathematics and Computer Science. I chose this course because I love the challenge of problem-solving and the way technology enhances learning. The intersection of math, coding, and education fascinates me, especially in fields like artificial intelligence and educational technology. Beyond academics, I’m passionate about coaching underage GAA and tutoring math, as they allow me to mentor and inspire young learners. My ultimate goal is to become an innovative educator who integrates technology into classrooms, making complex concepts more accessible and engaging for students.

People Who Inspire Me

Sal Khan

In the world of technology and education, I find inspiration in Sal Khan, the founder of Khan Academy. His vision of providing free, high-quality education to anyone, anywhere, has transformed how students engage with learning. His ability to simplify complex concepts and make education more accessible aligns with my own teaching philosophy.

Dr. Fei-Fei Li

Another person who greatly inspires me is Dr. Fei-Fei Li, a leading researcher in artificial intelligence and an advocate for diversity in STEM. Her groundbreaking work in AI and computer vision demonstrates how technology can be used ethically and responsibly.

Sir Ken Robinson

Lastly, Sir Ken Robinson, an influential educator and advocate for creativity in learning, has shaped my perspective on teaching. His work has encouraged me to think beyond conventional teaching methods and explore ways to make learning more interactive and inspiring.
